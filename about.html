Who I am.

An extrovert since birth, I'm curious about everything. I love to know and explore everything from travel, photography, food, pretty product packaging, people - I'm drawn to all matters of design and movement.

Shortly after our arrival in St. Paul, my parents moved us to Eden Prairie, Minnesota - a rural town that quickly grew into one of the fastest suburbs in the Twin Cities. A vast cultural difference from anything I had ever known. My upbrining in Eden Prarie inspired me to want to learn more about the world that I live in. I have a natural curosity in the diversity of people, my passion for wanting to understand the why's and how's and who's...this also explains my love of science fiction. I'm a true sci-fi geek at heart.

I have an double undergraduate degree in Marketing Management and Business Communication from the University of St. Thomas. I'm currently purusing a post-baccalaureate Interactive Design and Marketing Certificate from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. I now live in Phoenix, Arizona, and taking every opportunity I can to explore my new home in the desert. When I'm not working or studying, I love spending time with family and friends and giving my digital camera a workout.
